A DIGITAL CHESSBOARD

Take control of the Game

Rolling pawn is an affordable digital chess board designed to make learning chess much more effective and fun. This chess board helps you to record your games digitally as you play the real games in the physical board.

Watch Our Video duration: 1:00 min
Record games

Record your games played in the physical board in PGN format, and analyse your games.

Stockfish Engine

Play with various level of the latest stockfish engine and improve gradually.

Opensource

Keep getting the free updates of the amazing features added by the community.

Price

The complete cost of development of the board is less than $20. Easy steps to build it yourself.

Beautiful User Interface

A simple user interface to quickly get connected and start playing. Rolling pawn provides a very simple interface, so that you can just concentrate on improving your game.
